I'm indonesian and bahasa is my first language i already know since i was born. I was living in the environment that the people speak bahasa and sundanese language, hence both are the languages i'm able to speak till a day. When i grew up, i realize that english is one of the most important language i should learn, it's able to connect me with everyone around the world. People from Africa, Asia, Europe, etc use this language for communication with each other. Even though they already have their own mother tongue.

Till today i always improving my english skill at any way of communicating. I learn how to speak, read, listen, and writing in english, sometimes it's kind of overwhelming for me since i always think that i'm not a good learner. But i want to change my way of think, i want to be able to speak in english and communicate in it fluently so i'm able to get a job at overseas. I've watched several videos about learning english in youtube and also read some blogs i can get from google that there are so many insights i got.

This is one of the blog i write in english language, i want to make this blog my diary for me learning english. I won't write everything about english, but i will write everything about my passion in english. As i wrote in <a href={`${introUrl}/blog/introduction.md`}>introduction</a> my passion is about computer engineering. I'm a web programmer who passionate build something using my expertise, and this blog will be my starting point through the journey.

I know this wouldn't be easy for me to embrace english and take it as a part of my life. But i have to, cause if i want to improve my life i have to do what improves myself. This is the beginning of my journey, and i hope everyone who read this can get the inspiration or at least motivation to do more than what i did.
