I was living in the environment that the people speak bahasa and sundanese language, hence both are the languages i'm able to speak till a day. When i grew up, i realize that english is one of the most important language i should learn, it's able to connect me with everyone around the world. People from Africa, Asia, Europe, etc use this language for communication with each other. Even though they already have their own mother tongue.

Till today i always improving my english skill at any way of communicating. But i want to change my way of think, i want to be able to speak in english and communicate in it fluently so i'm able to get a job at overseas. I've watched several videos about learning english in youtube and also read some blogs i can get from google that there are so many insights i got.
