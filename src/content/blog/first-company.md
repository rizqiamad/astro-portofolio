## Beginning

I still remember the day when i got the email from the company, i was so excited and nervous at the same time. I couldn't believe that i got accepted, i mean i just graduated from senior high school and don't have any degree. But they accepted me, and i'm so grateful for that. I knew this company from friend of mine, when he told me that the company is hiring, then i immediately applied for it. I didn't expect much, i just want to try my luck.

Arriving to the office and got interviewed by the owner made me kind of nervous, my heart beats faster, but somehow it's relief knowing that i dare to deal with something like this. At the end of the interview, my boss asked me about the salary i'd ask for, and i said that i would accept even if it's unpaid. It's kind of naive, but at the moment he asked me, i just think that i just need a place to try and learn first before getting more salary. I knew that everything cannot be happened suddenly, it must be stack of occurences that related each other. At that time, i don't have any experience professionally, so i must trade something that i want to get something i need, in that case i traded my salary with the opportunity.

## Days of internship

In the middle of the day, i got chat from the owner that made me very happy, yes i got accepted and was able to start the first day at the next day. It was releafing internship time for me, because i got a lot of experiences there, the seniors gave a lot of advices about life, like how to deal with people, how to plan and achieve a good career, and many more. I learnt how to build an app that is usefull rather than building something perfect but nobody use it. I think this internship was the one that will boost my career, not just in technical way but also in mindset and ethic.

After several months work here as an intern, i tried to apply to another company as a fulltime employee. I just bet to my skill and cv, cause i don't have any degree to show off to the employer, as long as i try and never giving up, i believe that i would make it to achieve my goal. After applying several cv to several company, finally i got an schedule to get an interview. Continuing to homework test, then to another interview, alhamdulillah i got my first offering. Without any hesitation i accepted the offering and would be reallocated to jakarta for starting the work.

## Ending

So that's my short journey as an intern, the first step of my career. After 3 months of internship, now it's time to get another step further. I hope every knowledges and experiences here will help me through another challenges i'll face then. And i want to say thanks for the colleagues that help me and teach me how to be a better person and be a better employee.
